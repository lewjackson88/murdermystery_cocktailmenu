The Bedford Street Dossier

A Compendium of Barrowford's Potent Potions for the Discerning Investigator

Welcome, discerning guests, to a night of intrigue and delicious libations! Proceed with caution, for each sip may reveal a clue... or a motive.

1. The Scarlett Siren

This classic gets a femme fatale twist with its alluring red hue. A sweet and fruity concoction that's dangerously delicious, served frozen.

50ml white rum, 25ml fresh lime juice, 25ml strawberry liqueur (or syrup), 4-5 frozen strawberries, 1 tsp sugar (optional), Ice for blending

2. Bubblegum Bludgeon

A surprisingly sweet and vibrant concoction, but don't let its innocent appearance fool you! This whimsical daiquiri is a playful yet intriguing choice, served frozen.

50ml white rum, 25ml fresh lime juice, 25ml bubblegum syrup, Ice for blending

3. The Alibi Apple

Crisp, refreshing, and seemingly innocent â€“ just like a perfect alibi! This vodka-based drink is light, tart, and subtly floral.

50ml vodka, 100ml clear apple juice, 25ml fresh lime juice, 15ml elderflower cordial (optional), Ice

4. The Passionate Pursuit

A vibrant and tantalising cocktail, its exotic sweetness masks a thrilling pursuit for the truth. This modern classic is both intriguing and delightful.

50ml vodka, 25ml passion fruit liqueur (or puree/syrup), 15ml vanilla syrup, 15ml fresh lime juice, Shot of Prosecco (on the side), Ice

5. The Fatal Fizz

Light and effervescent, but with a dark secret lurking beneath the bubbles. This gin-based cocktail is bubbly, zesty, and effortlessly stylish.

50ml gin, 25ml fresh lemon juice, 15ml simple syrup, Soda water, Ice

6. The Minty Motive

A refreshing twist on a classic, this vibrant concoction hints at a cool, calculated plan. Fresh and invigorating, it leaves no room for doubt.

50ml white rum, 25ml fresh lime juice, 2 tsp sugar, 8-10 fresh mint leaves, Soda water, Ice

Enjoy your drinks, and good luck solving the mystery!
